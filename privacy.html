Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Florant Operating System, trading through Florantops, collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information. Florantops is built for African businesses, particularly businesses in South Africa.

1. Information we collect

When you contact Florantops, book a demo, request pricing, sign up, or send a message through the website, we may collect personal details such as your name, email address, phone number, bakery name, business type, number of staff, preferred demo date, and message.

2. How we use information

We use this information to respond to enquiries, book demos, provide pricing information, communicate with you, support customers, send account or service messages, and improve the Florantops service.

3. Florantops system data

Florantops may store bakery operating data such as sales, stock, expenses, receivables, production records, staff roles, reports, customer information, and other system information. This data is stored through Supabase, which provides database and hosting infrastructure for the service.

4. Supabase and service providers

We use Supabase to store and manage system data. Supabase may process information only as needed to provide hosting, database, security, and related technical services. We may also use service providers for email, support, payment processing, or hosting. We do not sell personal information.

5. Payment information

Florantops does not intend to store full card details or banking details in its website or Supabase database. If online payment is enabled, payment details should be handled by the payment provider, and Florantops may store only payment status, subscription status, transaction references, and related billing records needed to operate the service.

6. Cookies and tracking

Florantops does not currently use cookies, Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, WhatsApp tracking, or other marketing tracking tools on this website. If that changes, this policy will be updated.

7. Legal basis and POPIA

For South African users, Florantops aims to handle personal information in line with the Protection of Personal Information Act, 2013 (POPIA). We collect and use information for legitimate business purposes, to respond to requests, to provide services, and to manage customer relationships.

8. Data security

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information and system data from loss, misuse, unauthorised access, disclosure, or alteration. No online service can guarantee perfect security, but we aim to use practical safeguards appropriate for the service.

9. Your rights

You may ask us to access, correct, or delete your personal information by contacting us at Info@florantops.com. We may need to verify your identity before processing the request.

10. Retention

We keep personal information only for as long as needed for business, support, legal, accounting, or service purposes, unless a longer period is required or allowed by law.

11. Contact

For privacy questions, contact Florantops at Info@florantops.com, call 060 313 3433 or 065 671 3041, or write to 2879 Roets Drive, Vosloorus, Johannesburg.
