Pricing FAQ

Which plan should a small bakery start with?

Most small bakeries with daily sales should start with Bakery Core because it adds stock alerts, production tracking, supplier costs, customer accounts, and a daily operating report.

Can home bakeries use Florantops?

Yes. Starter is designed for home bakeries, side bakeries, and very small shops that need daily sales, cash-up, expenses, simple stock, receivables, and basic reports.

Can I add another branch later?

Yes. Multi-Branch supports 2 outlets, and extra branches can be added for R150-R200/month per additional shop or location.

Is there a yearly discount?

Yes. Yearly billing gives a 10% discount on every Florantops pricing tier, including extra branch add-ons. Yearly subscriptions are paid upfront.

Can I see Florantops before choosing a plan?

Yes. The best next step is to book a demo and review the workflow with your bakery in mind.
