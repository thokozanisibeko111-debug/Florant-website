Pricing FAQ

Why is pricing quote-based?

Bakery needs vary by order volume, staff count, modules, and onboarding support. A quote helps match the plan to the real workflow.

Can small bakeries use Florantops?

Yes. Florantops can support home bakers, small bakeries, cake businesses, wholesale bakeries, and growing teams.

Can I see Florantops before choosing a plan?

Yes. The best next step is to book a demo and review the workflow with your bakery in mind.
