Common Questions

Is Florantops only for large bakeries?

No. Florantops can be used by home bakers, small bakeries, cake businesses, wholesale bakeries, and growing bakery teams.

Can Florantops help with custom cake orders?

Yes. Florantops can help capture customer details, cake specifications, due dates, notes, and order status.

Does Florantops help with ingredient stock?

Yes. Florantops can help track ingredients, packaging, stock usage, and low-stock items.

Can Florantops help me calculate product costs?

Yes. By connecting recipes and ingredient costs, Florantops can help you understand the cost of making each product.

Do I need technical skills to use Florantops?

No. Florantops should be simple and practical for bakery owners and teams.
