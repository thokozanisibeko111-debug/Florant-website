Florantops Works for Every Type of Bakery Business

From home bakers to wholesale production teams, Florantops helps bakery businesses organize their daily workflow and grow with better systems.

Retail Bakeries

Manage daily production, walk-in orders, inventory, product costing, sales reports, and front-of-house communication.

Custom Cake Businesses

Track customer notes, cake specifications, flavours, due dates, deposits, design instructions, pickup, and delivery details.

Wholesale Bakeries

Organize recurring orders, bulk production, delivery planning, receivables, invoicing, and ingredient usage at scale.

Home Bakeries

Move away from scattered notes and messages with a system for orders, recipes, customers, pricing, and growth.

Support consistent recipes, stock visibility, production standards, team roles, and reporting across multiple bakery locations.

Bring recipe-based production, batch planning, stock control, costing, and team tasks into one practical workflow.
