About Florantops

Florantops was created to help bakery businesses move away from scattered systems, manual processes, and operational confusion.

Mission

Less chasing information. More growing the bakery.

Our goal is to give bakeries a simple, reliable way to manage orders, production, recipes, stock, costing, cash flow, and performance. Bakery owners should spend less time fighting admin and more time building a strong, profitable operation.

Practical

Florantops focuses on the real work bakeries do every day, from sales and custom orders to production and cash movement.

Organized

Information should be easy to find, easy to update, and clear enough for the whole team to use.

Built to grow

The system supports better habits for bakeries moving from manual operations into stronger digital control.

Our story

Built around the real problems bakeries face every day

Florantops started with a simple observation: too many bakeries run on a mix of notebooks, spreadsheets, and WhatsApp messages — and that is where orders slip, costs blur, and busy days turn stressful. We set out to bring those scattered pieces into one clear system designed for how bakeries actually work, from the first order to the final report.

Today we focus on building practical tools that bakery owners and their teams can trust every single day, so they can spend less time chasing information and more time baking, selling, and growing.
